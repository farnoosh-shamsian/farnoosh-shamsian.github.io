Hi! I'm Farnoosh

I am an Iranian researcher in Philology at Leipzig University in Germany. My research interests include digital humanities, classical philology, historical language pedagogy, translation alignment, and the reception of ancient Greek texts in Persian literature. My primary focus is on the localization of historical language pedagogy in Persian, and the reception of Ancient Greek text in the Persian language.
