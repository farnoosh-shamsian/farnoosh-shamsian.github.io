Hi! I'm Farnoosh

I am an Iranian doctoral candidate in Ancient History at Leipzig University in Germany. My research interests include digital humanities, classical philology, historical language pedagogy, translation alignment, and the reception of ancient Greek texts in Persian literature. My primary focus is on localization of historical language pedagogy in Persian, and teaching Ancient Greek through authentic Greek texts.
